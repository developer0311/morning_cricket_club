M. C. C. L Trophy Cricket Tournament - Rules & Regulations

  • 1. No arguments will be entertained regarding the umpire's decision.
  • 2. Misbehavior or abusive conduct from any player is strictly prohibited.
  • 3. Each match will be played with 6 overs per side.
  • 4. In every match, one bowler may bowl a maximum of 2 overs. The remaining 4 overs must be bowled by 4 different bowlers (1 over each).
  • 5. All players must report to the ground by 4:30 PM on Sunday, 25th May 2025.
  • 6. Use of offensive or inappropriate language on the field is strictly not allowed.
  • 7. Dinner arrangements will be made for all players on match day.
  • 8. Players are not allowed to wear slippers while playing.
